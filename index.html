lasagna dish recipe

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that consists of multiple layers of pasta sheets, sauce, cheese, and often additional ingredients such as meat,
vegetables, and herbs. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is often baked in the oven until the top layer becomes golden and bubbly.

ingredients

  • 9 lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • /2 pound (225g) Italian sausage (casings removed)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • /4 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • 2 cups (500g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. in a large skillet, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Break up the meat into crumbles as it cooks.
    Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
    Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. While the sauce simmers, prepare the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese. Mix well.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles,
    followed by a layer of the meat sauce. Repeat these layers until all the noodles are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.
  10. Cover the baking dish with foil, ensuring it doesn't touch the cheese. Bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and golden brown.
  12. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  13. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired, and serve warm.
